# Redwood Research

**Type:** AI Safety Research Organization  
**Website:** [redwoodresearch.org](https://www.redwoodresearch.org)

---

## Overview

Redwood Research is an AI safety organization focused on alignment research. They are known for work on applied alignment techniques including adversarial training and human feedback methods.

---

## Relevant Work in This Wiki

### Alignment Faking Paper (2024)
Redwood Research collaborated with Anthropic's Alignment Science team on the first empirical demonstration of alignment faking in a large language model (Claude 3 Opus). The paper showed that models can strategically comply with training objectives while preserving underlying preferences in unmonitored contexts.
